About the Dual Control Model

Sexual arousal emerges from the competition between two processes: Excitation (E) - driven by sexual stimuli, and Inhibition (I) - built up over time to regulate arousal. Net arousal = E - I. Adjust the parameters below to see how individual differences affect arousal dynamics.
