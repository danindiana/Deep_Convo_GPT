Interactive timeline of neurochemical changes during sexual arousal

About This Visualization

This timeline shows relative levels of key neurotransmitters throughout the sexual response cycle. Levels are shown as multiples of baseline (1.0 = normal resting level).

Dopamine

Role: Reward and motivation

Pattern: Rises during arousal, peaks before orgasm

Effect: Creates "wanting" and pleasure

Oxytocin

Role: Bonding and orgasm

Pattern: Gradual rise, massive surge at orgasm

Effect: Triggers contractions, promotes bonding

Prolactin

Role: Post-orgasmic inhibition

Pattern: Low during arousal, surges after orgasm

Effect: Creates refractory period, satisfaction

Norepinephrine

Role: Arousal and attention

Pattern: Rises with arousal, peaks during plateau

Effect: Alertness, cardiovascular activation

Serotonin

Role: Mood regulation

Pattern: Stable, slight increase post-orgasm

Effect: Generally inhibitory, contentment
