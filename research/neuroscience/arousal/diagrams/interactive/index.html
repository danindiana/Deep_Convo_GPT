About the Research

These visualizations are part of a comprehensive neuroscience research project exploring the mechanisms of sexual arousal. The project integrates findings from neuroimaging, endocrinology, psychology, and computational modeling to provide a multi-level understanding of arousal processes.

Key Components:

  • Interactive Mermaid diagrams documenting neural pathways
  • Comprehensive glossary with 100+ neuroscience terms
  • Annotated literature review of 30+ peer-reviewed papers
  • Python computational models of arousal dynamics
  • Interactive Jupyter notebooks for hands-on exploration
  • These interactive web visualizations
